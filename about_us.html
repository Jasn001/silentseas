About Us

Silent Seas is a dedicated initiative by SaveSilentSeas.org focused on combating plastic pollution in our oceans. We believe in the power of collective action to protect marine ecosystems and the creatures that inhabit them. Our mission is to raise awareness, educate communities, and drive impactful solutions to address the plastic crisis. Together, we can create a cleaner, healthier ocean for future generations.
